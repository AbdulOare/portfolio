I'm a Senior Technical Writer and Documentation Architect with over eight years of experience turning complex systems into documentation that people actually use. I've worked across fintech, banking, SaaS, and enterprise middleware — from building documentation units from scratch to shipping user manuals for systems serving thousands of customers across Africa.

My work spans the full documentation lifecycle: information architecture, style guide creation, cross-functional collaboration with developers and product teams, and the hands-on writing itself. I believe good documentation is a product decision, not an afterthought, and I've spent my career making the case for that — and proving it.

Currently working as a Middleware Documentation Engineer at Afreximbank via Revent Technologies, documenting enterprise integration systems across Fiorano, Boomi, Kafka, and SFTP platforms. Previously, I led the Technical Writing unit at Access Bank PLC — Africa's largest bank — where I built the team, defined the workflows, and created the documentation infrastructure from nothing.
